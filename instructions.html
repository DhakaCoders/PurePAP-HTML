READ AND FOLLOW ALL INSTRUCTIONS FOR SAFE AND EFFECTIVE CLEANING OF CPAP HOSE, MASK & WATER RESERVOIR. FAILURE TO FOLLOW INSTRUCTIONS CAN CAUSE SERIOUS INJURY. SAVE ALL PACKAGING, INSTRUCTIONS AND COMPONENTS INCLUDED WITH THIS PRODUCT.

PurePapTM is intended to clean Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) Hose, Mask and Water Reservoir using bacteria and germ killing activated oxygen (Ozone). It is intended for home use. It's recommended that you clean your CPAP Hose and Mask daily. Water Reservoir should be cleaned weekly. Discontinue use of PurePapTM if acne or rash develops along mask contact line. PurePapTM will not remove skin oils and mucus. To remove these fluids, you can wipe down your equipment with PurePapTM CPAP wipes or other methods of cleaning as directed by your equipment manufacture.
  • Keep away from children.
  • At elevated concentrations in the air, the ozone produced by PurePapTM is harmful to health. Never breathe directly from your PurePapTM machine.
  • Person's with acute or chronic respiratory conditions such as asthma, bronchitis and sensitive or irritated breathing passages (mouth, nose, throat) should consult with their medical doctor before using this product.
  • Never use PurePapTM in confined spaces less than 120 square foot (10' x 12') room with 8' ceiling.
  • Never operate the PurePapTM when its not unattached to CPAP machine or Sanitizing Bag. If a strong odor is detected when PurePapTM is operating, turn off PurePapTM by pressing the on/off button. Check hose connections and make sure there are no cracks in the housing, CPAP hose and hose adapters.
  • Keep PurePapTM away from all water sources.
  • PurePapTM can be cleaned with damp cloth.
  • Keep PurePapTM away from flammable chemicals and products that produce flammable gasses.
  • Empty your water reservoir before cleaning.
  • Do not cover PurePapTM or block air intake vent.
  • Do not run PurePapTM more than 30 minutes consecutively.
  • Do not run PurePapTM when charging cord is attached to a power source. This may impact battery life.
  • PurePapTM is only to be used to clean your CPAP machine, hoses and mask.
  • Do not dismantle PurePapTM.

FAQ

How often should I clean my CPAP machine? You should clean hose and mask daily. CPAP machine water reservoir should be cleaned weekly.
What type of USB power adapter works with PurePapTM? Any 5 Volt 1 Amp USB adapter that charges your cell phone or other small electronics can be used.
What if I don't have a USB power adapter? You can use a charging port on wall or computer or purchase an adapter on Amazon.com or at your local retailer. USB Power Adapter Specification: 100 - 240 Volt, 50-60 MHz, 5 Volt 1 Amp Output
Do I need to use the large Cleaning Zip Bag when cleaning CPAP machine hose, mask and water reservoir? Yes. This is required for effective cleaning and safe operation of your PurePapTM
Can I travel with PurePapTM on airlines? The power capacity of the lithium ion battery meets FAA/TSA requirements. PurePapTM can't be packed in stored luggage during travel. PurePapTM must be carried onto plane.
What is the blue bag with zipper for? The blue bag serves as a storage bag for PurePapTM unit, accessories, CPAP Hose and Mask when not in use or when you travel.
